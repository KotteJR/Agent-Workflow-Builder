# REGULATION (EU) No. 2024/1143

**Date:** April 11, 2024

**Subject:** Amendments to Regulation (EU) No. 1308/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council concerning the common organisation of the markets in agricultural products

## GENERAL PROVISIONS

### Purpose

This Regulation amends Regulation (EU) No. 1308/2013 to establish updated rules for the common organisation of the markets in agricultural products and introduces modifications to facilitate the implementation of the Common Agricultural Policy (CAP).

## KEY AMENDMENTS

### Market Support Measures

This Regulation introduces modifications to various market support measures including:

- Enhanced crisis management mechanisms for agricultural producers
- Updated support systems for specific agricultural sectors
- Improved market transparency and information systems

### Agricultural Sectors Affected

The amendments apply to the following agricultural sectors:

1. **Cereals** - Modifications to support and intervention mechanisms
2. **Rice** - Updated conversion coefficients and processing rules
3. **Olive Oil and Olives** - Revised support arrangements
4. **Fruit and Vegetables** - Enhanced producer organization rules
5. **Wine** - Updated oenological practices and market measures
6. **Beef and Sheepmeat** - Modified intervention and support mechanisms
7. **Dairy** - Updated milk market support measures

### Implementation Timeline

The amendments become effective on the date of publication in the Official Journal of the European Union and apply from the marketing year following adoption.

## ARTICLE AMENDMENTS

### Crisis Management Provisions

The Regulation strengthens the ability of the Commission to respond to market crises through:

- Expanded toolkit for crisis measures
- Expedited decision-making procedures
- Enhanced market monitoring capabilities

### Market Transparency

New provisions require:

- Enhanced price reporting from member states
- Real-time market information systems
- Improved data collection and dissemination

### Support for Agricultural Operators

Updated provisions include:

- Adjusted support levels for specified sectors
- New eligibility criteria for market support schemes
- Enhanced environmental and sustainability requirements

## TRANSITIONAL PROVISIONS

### Application of Previous Rules

Where not explicitly amended by this Regulation, the provisions of Regulation (EU) No. 1308/2013 shall continue to apply.

### Member State Adjustments

Member States shall adapt their national implementing legislation within the timeframes specified in this Regulation.

### Financial Adjustments

Budget allocations and financing mechanisms shall be adjusted according to the modified market support levels established in this Regulation.

## COMPETENCE AND DELEGATION

### Commission Powers

The Commission shall have authority to:

- Adopt implementing acts to specify application procedures
- Establish detailed rules for market measures
- Determine administrative requirements
- Issue guidance on Member State implementation

### Delegated Acts

Where appropriate, the Commission may adopt delegated acts under the conditions established in Article 227 of Regulation (EU) No. 1308/2013 for:

- Technical amendments to market support mechanisms
- Adjustments to support levels
- Updates to implementation procedures

## REPORTING AND EVALUATION

### Member State Obligations

Member States shall:

- Report on implementation of amended provisions
- Provide market data as required
- Submit producer information reports
- Comply with monitoring requirements

### Commission Evaluations

The Commission shall:

- Monitor implementation across Member States
- Evaluate effectiveness of amended measures
- Report to European Parliament and Council
- Propose further modifications as necessary

### Evaluation Schedule

Comprehensive reviews shall be conducted:

- Annually for crisis measures
- Every three years for market support mechanisms
- Every seven years for overall policy effectiveness

## FINAL PROVISIONS

### Entry into Force

This Regulation shall enter into force on the day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.

### Application

This Regulation shall apply from the agricultural marketing year following its adoption, unless otherwise specified in individual articles.

### Transitional Arrangements

Any provisions of Regulation (EU) No. 1308/2013 that are superseded by this Regulation shall cease to apply on the effective dates specified herein.

### Repeal and Amendments

This Regulation amends Regulation (EU) No. 1308/2013. In the event of conflict between provisions, this Regulation shall take precedence for the matters it addresses.
