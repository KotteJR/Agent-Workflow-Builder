# REGULATION (EC) No. 1334/2008

**Date:** December 16, 2008

**Subject:** On flavouring substances and certain food ingredients with flavouring properties used in and on foods and amending Regulation (EEC) No. 1601/91 of the Council, Regulations (EC) No. 2232/96 and (EC) No. 110/2008 and Directive 2000/13/EC

## CHAPTER I - OBJECT, SCOPE AND DEFINITIONS

## ARTICLE 1 - Object

### Purpose

This Regulation establishes rules for flavouring substances and food ingredients with flavouring properties used in and on foods, with a view to ensuring the effective functioning of the internal market while at the same time ensuring a high level of human health protection and a high level of consumer protection, including the protection of consumer interests and fair food trade practices, and taking into account, where it is deemed necessary, environmental protection.

### Specific Objectives

For these purposes, this Regulation provides for:

**a)** A Community list of flavouring substances and source materials approved for use in and on foods, set out in Annex I (hereinafter referred to as "the Community list")

**b)** Conditions of use for flavouring substances and food ingredients with flavouring properties in and on foods

**c)** Rules on the labelling of flavouring substances

## ARTICLE 2 - Scope

### 1. Application

This Regulation shall apply to the following:

**a)** Flavouring substances used or intended to be used in and on foods, subject to more specific provisions provided for in Regulation (EC) No. 2065/2003

**b)** Food ingredients with flavouring properties

**c)** Foods containing flavouring substances and/or food ingredients with flavouring properties

**d)** Source materials for flavouring substances and/or source materials for food ingredients with flavouring properties

### 2. Non-Application

This Regulation shall not apply to:

**a)** Substances with exclusively sweet, sour or salty taste

**b)** Unprocessed foods

**c)** Non-compound foods and mixtures such as fresh, dried or frozen spices and/or herbs, tea mixtures and mixtures for infusions as such, provided they have not been used as food ingredients
